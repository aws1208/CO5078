About This Project

Game of Thrones: Manchester is a fan-made reimagining of the Seven Kingdoms set within the iconic streets, canals, and districts of Manchester. It blends the gritty industrial heritage of the city with the political intrigue, ancient rivalries, and looming threats that define the world of Westeros.

This project began as a playful thought experiment: What if the North of England were the true North? From that idea grew a full reinterpretation—where Deansgate becomes King’s Landing, Castlefield stands as Winterfell reborn, and the roar of Old Trafford echoes like dragons overhead.

Every location, house, and event is crafted to reflect both the lore of Game of Thrones and the unmistakable character of Manchester. Rain-soaked cobbles, red-brick fortresses, and the ever-present chill of northern weather make the city a perfect stage for this alternate realm.

This site is not affiliated with HBO, George R. R. Martin, or any official Game of Thrones property. It is simply a creative tribute to two worlds that share a love of history, drama, and the unbreakable spirit of the North.
